About Us

Our Mission

We are proud to have a broad clientele of satisfied Malayalee’s across the globe. With more than 4 decades of optical experience, we pride ourselves in giving the best service for every visual need.

We are the first opticians in South India to get the prestigious ISO 9001:2008 certification from BVQI, the reputed international inspection agency. Our vision is to offer the highest quality eye care to our customers.

Our Values

At Lens & Frames, we strive to push the boundaries of innovation in eye-care. We are passionate about providing the best to our customers and believe in offering value for money with every service we provide.

We offer a large collection of India and international brands, and our staff is always ready to meet customized requests from our loyal customers.
